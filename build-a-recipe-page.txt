    <h1>Coffee Receipe</h1>
    <p> A receipe on how to make a Mocha</p>
    <h2>Ingredients</h2>
    <ul>
    <li>100ml Espresso</li>
    <li>250ml Milk</li>
    <li>4 pumps of Mocha Syrup</li>
    <li>Whipped Cream (optional)</li>
    </ul>
    <h2>Instructions</h2>
    <ol>
    <li>Grind the coffee beans</li>
    <li> Add the grinded beans to the coffee machine</li>
      <li>Begin steaming the milk</li>
      <li>Add the 4 Pumps of mocha syrup to the cup</li>
      <li>Add the esspresso and milk to the cup, add whipped cream if requested</li>
      </ol>
